About Deturgent

Det-Urgent aims to be a major player in the green business industry, specializing in detergent products, however the reasoning behind this goes beyond simply searching for profit. The company also has an agenda to increase the awareness of how important green business is to the surrounding environment, moreover, the company has the agenda of taking advantage of local resources, particularly Belimbing Wuluh.
